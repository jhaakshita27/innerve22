About The Event

Innerve is the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW). With its inception in 2015, every year, the two-day extravaganza has witnessed a phenomenol gathering of students from all domains. The event is back for its FIFTH edition where it aims to be a platform for participants to test their engineering skills and showcase their prowess in the VIRTUAL mode.

Where

IGDTUW, New Delhi

When

Monday to Wednesday
4-6 October'22

F.A.Q

  • What is Innerve?

    Innerve is the annual technical fest of one of the most prestigious college, Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women(IGDTUW) of Delhi.

  • How can I get regular updates about Innerve?

    You can subscribe to our newsletter and follow us on our social media handles given below, to get regular updates about Innerve.

  • Is there a fee required for event registeration?

    All the events don't have a registeration fees. It depends on which event you want to register.

  • How do I pay for the registration?

    You can make an online payment in any of the given modes: Debit/credit cards, internet banking, mobile wallets, digital payment apps and Unified Payments Interface (UPI) service.
